If you copy the V5 directory from the C drive, say, to the D drive there are three files that you must edit for the program to run.
  1. \V5\Data\Computer.csv
  2. \V5\Exes\Startup.lst
  3. \V5\Exes\Visitrak.ini

You will also have to right click on the TrueTrak icon, choose Properties, and change the path from C:\V5\Exes\Su.exe to D:\V5\Exes\Su.exe.
