How to Install the HP8100N Network Printer

  1. Press Start in the lower left corner
  2. Choose Settings
  3. Choose Printers
  4. Double Click Add Printer
  5. Network Printer
  6. Press Brouse
  7. Brouse to visitrak_org
  8. Choose HPLJ8100_Q
  9. Press Have Disk
  10. Brouse to F:\Public\HP8100drv and press Ok
  11. This will automatically select HP8100N Postscript 6
  12. Press OK
