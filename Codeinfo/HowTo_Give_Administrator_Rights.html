1. Log onto the PC as the Domain Administrator
  2. Right click My Computer and choose Manage
  3. Open the Local Users and Groups folder
  4. Open the Groups folder
  5. Double click Administrators
  6. Click the Add button
  7. Enter the user's domain logon (first initial + last name)
  8. Click Ok and Ok again to exit
  9. Log off the PC and logon as the user. You should now have full rights.

Don't log on a workstation as the logal administrator. Use the domain administrator account as this as administrator rights to all workstations that are members of the domain.
