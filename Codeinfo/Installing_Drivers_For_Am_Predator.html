Installing the American Preditor Drivers for Windows 95

Right click on My Computer and choose Properties.
Click on the Device Manager tab.
If you don't have the Intel chipset drivers installed you will see a large question mark. If you click on the + sign to the left of the question mark you will see three smaller question marks labeled
PCI Bridge
PCI Ethernet Controller
PCI Universal Serial Bus

  • If you do not have one already, create a C:\Drivers folder. Put the chipset floppy into the floppy drive and copy the Chipset and Ethernet folders to the C:\Drivers folder.
  • Put the Win 95 VGA for Am Predator floppy into the floppy drive and copy the W95500.exe file to the C:\Drivers folder.

Installing the Chipset Drivers

  • Run C:\Drivers\Chipset\setup.exe
  • At the Welcome screen, press Next>.
  • At the licence screen, press Yess.
  • Readme: Press Next>
  • Destination Location : Next>
  • Actions : Next>
  • Setup Complete. Choose Yes I want to restart my computer now and press OK
  • After the computer reboots you will get a series of "New Hardware Found" messages. These will stop at the Update Device Driver Wizard Press Next>
  • It will find the driver and you have only to press Finish.
  • You will next be told that it can't find a file for the USB Controller. Press Skip File
  • You will be asked for another file for the USB Controller. Press Skip File again.
  • When asked if you want to reboot, answer Yes.
  • After rebooting, the computer will find New Hardware again. This time it will be the Primary IDE Controller. When it asks if you want to reboot, answer No
  • The computer will find the Secondary IDE Controller this time. When it asks if you want to reboot, answer Yes.
  • Right click on My Computer and choose Properties. Click on the Device Manager tab
  • Under Universal Serial Bus Controller, click once on the Intel(r) 82371AB... PCI to USB Universal Host Controller and then click the Properties button at the bottom of the screen. Under Device Usage, click the Disable in this hardware profile checkbox.
  • Under ? Other Devices you will find the ?PCI Ethernet Controller. Click once on this and then press the Properties button. Click on the Driver tab and press the Update Driver button.
    When asked "Do you want Windows to Search" select Yes. It will come back with "Unable to find". Press Browse and browse to C:\Drivers\Ethernet and press OK and then OK. It will come back with "Found drivers for Intel...". Press Finish.
    You will be asked to Insert Disk. Click OK. It will come up with a Copying Files dialog looking for R100ent.sys. Browse to C:\Drivers\Ethernet again and then OK, OK. You will then be asked to provide a comptuer and workgroup name. Enter W01 and VISITRAK.
    When you click Close the computer will ask for the Windows 95 CD. Pull down the listbox and choose C:\WIN95 and press OK.
    Restart. When asked for a username and password enter W01 with no password. Press OK. When asked for passwords again just press OK.
  • Right Click on the Network Neighborhood and choose Properties. Remove the Client for Netware Networks and the IPX/SPX-Compatible protocol.
  • Press the Add button. Click Protocol. Press Add. Click Microsoft on the left and then TCP/IP on the right. Press Ok
  • Press the File and Printer Sharing button. Check the I want to be able to give others access to my files. Press OK. When asked to insert the disk, choose C:\WIN95.
  • Remove the NetBEUI Protocol.
  • Configure TCP/IP.
  • Restart
  • Right click the Network Neighborhood again and choose Properties. Pull down the Primary Network Login listbox and choose Windows Login. Press OK. You do not have to restart after this.
