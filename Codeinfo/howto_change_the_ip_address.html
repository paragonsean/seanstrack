How to Change the IP addresss on a True-Trak 2020 ™ with Two Ethernet Ports

Background:

The new True-Trak 2020™ systems have two ethernet ports. One of these is connected directly to the FasTrak21™ Board and the other can be connected to a network.

There are three ethernet ports involved here.
  Ethernet PortCurrent TCP/IP AddressDesired TCP/IP Address
1 FasTrak2™ Board 192.168.254.99 10.172.16.99
2 Computer FasTrak2 Direct Connection 192.168.254.100 10.172.16.100
3 Computer Local Area Connection Auto 192.168.254.100

Procedure:

You must change the FasTrak2™ Board address first. Then you can change the addresses of the computer's ports.

  1. The procedure for changing the FasTrak2™ Board address is described in the FasTrak2 help. On the Plot screen, pull down the Help menu and choose FasTrak2 Help.
  2. On the bottom right of the screen, under Misc., select How to Change the IP address of the Board
  3. Press the Click Here for Instructions button.
  4. Follow the directions to change the address of the board to 10.172.16.99.
  5. Shut down the True-Trak 2020™ software.
  6. Open the Windows Control Pannel and double click on Network Connections.
  7. Right Click on the FasTrak2 Direct Connection and choose Properties.
  8. Click once on the Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) line to highlight it and then press the Properties button.
  9. Change the IP Address to 10.172.16.100. Press the OK button to save the changes.
  10. Press the OK button on the properties screen to close that one.
  11. Right Click on the Local Area Connection and choose Properties.
  12. Click once on the Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) line to highlight it and then press the Properties button.
  13. Click on the Use the following IP address radio button.
  14. Change the IP Address to 192.168.254.100.
  15. Change the Subnet Mask to 255.255.255.0 or whatever your network requires.
  16. Press the OK button to save the changes.
  17. Press the OK button on the properties screen to close that one.
  18. Restart Windows.

Rules:

1. The first three numbers of the TCP/IP adresses of the ethernet ports on the FasTrak2™ Board and the FasTrak2 Direct Connection must be the same. The fourth number must be different.

Ethernet PortTCP/IP Address
FasTrak2™ Board10.172.16.99
FasTrak2™ Direct Connection10.172.16.100

2. The first three numbers of the TCP/IP addresses of the ethernet ports on the FasTrak2 Direct Connection and the Local Area Connection must be different;

Ethernet PortTCP/IP Address
FasTrak2™ Direct Connection10.172.16.100
Local Area Connection192.168.254.100

3. The Local Area Connection ethernet port can be set to Obtain the IP address automatically. This will not affect the communications with the FasTrak2™ Board as long as the first three numbers of the TCP/IP Addresses on your network are not the same as the ones on the FasTrak2™ Board and FasTrak2 Direct Connection ethernet ports.
